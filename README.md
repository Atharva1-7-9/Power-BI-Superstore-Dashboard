Project Objective
To analyze retail sales data and extract meaningful business insights such as:

- Top-performing product categories
- Customer segment contribution
- Regional sales and profit trends
- Profitability analysis across regions and products
- Monthly sales trend analysis

This helps understand market behavior and identify business growth opportunities.

---

Key Insights
- Highest sales come from the **Consumer segment**
- **Technology** and **Office Supplies** are top revenue-generating categories

- Clear seasonal trends in monthly sales
